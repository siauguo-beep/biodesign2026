The fracture

  • Extinction removes species and ecological function.
  • It erodes shared memory: shifting baselines, de-extinction hype.
  • We lose temporal coexistence: migrations, synchrony, rhythms.

Design questions

  • Translate extinction–absence into perceivable, debatable experience?
  • AI + biological data for disciplined public memory?
  • Land in accountability — not only immersion?
