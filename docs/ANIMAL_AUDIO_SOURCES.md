# Animal Audio Sources

These files power the WebXR species-card sound buttons. All entries are real recordings from Wikimedia Commons. Most prototype species are extinct, so `proxy` means the original species has no known surviving recording in this prototype and a related extant animal recording is used instead. No synthetic audio is used.
