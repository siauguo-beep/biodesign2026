## What Winners Have in Common

**Real science + speculative vision** — grounded in literature, but push into provocative futures

**Emotional hook** — makes you feel something (war, waste, sentimentality)

**Polished visuals** — this is a design competition, presentation quality is everything

**Systemic thinking** — who benefits, who's harmed, lifecycle, equity

**Honest reflection** — judges reward intellectual honesty over false confidence

**Small focused teams win** — 2024 winner was just 2 people

**Our edge:** MDes students who build with code (Cursor), design with AI, and think like designers.
